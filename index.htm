About me

I am currently a postdoc at the National University of Singapore (NUS), working with Prof. Mun Choon Chan and Prof. Bryan Low.

Previously I graduated from the Australian National University (Canberra) in December 2016. My PhD supervisor was Prof. Wray Buntine in the Monash University, Melbourne.
